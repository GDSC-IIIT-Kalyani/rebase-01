Unleash your creativity and be part of Rebase<01>, a month-long online hackathon extravaganza! Compete across various categories, showcasing your skills, creativity, and teamwork alongside peers who share your passion. This is your chance to challenge yourself, learn new skills, and create remarkable projects that can make a real difference. Don't miss this unparalleled opportunity to rebase your talent and elevate your skills to a new level. Step into Rebase<01>—where creativity has no bounds.

Literally anyone, student(graduate, undergraduate, high-school), professional, and almost anyone we forgot to mention here.
You can participate individually or in teams of 2 to 4 members. If you are participating in the hackathon individually and looking for a team, we will help you in connecting to other individual participants to get you a team.
Rebase<01> will be conducted in complete online mode, so you can participate from anywhere without any worry.
Rebase<01> is absolutely free inclusive of resources.
No prerequisites are required to participate in this hackathon. This event is open to participants of all skill levels.
Rebase <01> welcomes everyone! It is an inclusive open to all, so anyone can join in.
Submit a good application 🙂 Make sure your essays are good, that is how you move ahead. We will have:(i) An initial screening round to filter non-serious candidates and spam applications.(ii) A devfolio screening round where participants from round 1 will submit proposals and will get selected.
It is for EVERYBODY!! We will have mentors and resources available, along with several workshops targeted for beginners.
